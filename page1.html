BICIYES PRIVATE LIMITED 



     BICIYES Private Limited is a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013 in the state

 of Karnataka with its registered office in Bangalore.The Authorised share capital of the Company is 

INR 10 lakhs divided into 1 lakh equity shares of Rs. 10/- each. The Paid-up share capital of the 

Company is INR 1 lakh divided in to 10,000 equity share of Rs. 10/- each.
 


The promoters of the Company are Mr. Chandra Sekhara Reddy Battula and Mrs. Praveena Battula. The 

Company is engaged in the business of providing consultancy and advisory services in various fields, 

general, administrative, secretarial, commercial, financial, legal, economic, management, labour, 

industrial, public relations, scientific, technical, direct and indirect taxation and other levies, 

statistical, accountancy, quality control and data management.
